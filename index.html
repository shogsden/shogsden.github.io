Social Justice

Noel Cazenave

My friends and family will testify that I have a huge passion to see society, indivudals and communities create equality, justice and treatment based on people's characters; not on a socially constructed, judged and assumed identity. This is why I campaign for equality in all areas of life: DEI, Social Media, Demonstrations, Education and Personal Study!

The need for societal and idividual change is a lifelong practice. Bias that we have unconsciously learnt, microaggressions that we don't understand and overt behaviours that we display. All these incorporate how we treat and make others feel. Many things have been deeply embedded from the world in which we live - our hearts, our subconscious, our psychology, our unconscious, our thoughts, our souls, our minds and our behaviours. We must each strive to understand what we may do to invalidate and oppress people; and create painful experiences - this requires an open mind; letting our guards down; listening, reading and watching about others' experiences in life - from there, we can look to interact with one another in an upstanding way.

I openly stand and go further than just internal reflection. I seek to overhaul pain and oppression that has been deeply entrenched in our systems in every part of society. I am actively doing this by participating with Maastricht Kan Het - an anti-racist organisation for the local community.
